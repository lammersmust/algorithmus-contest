Judges Commission

The results of the participants will be evaluated by the judges commission which includes the world’s leading experts in the field of application development. The selection was carried out according to the following criteria:

  • Strong experience in software engineering no less than 4 years
  • Implementation of international projects as software developer and DevOps
  • Team leading of software development
  • Experience of IT mentoring and coaching
  • Experience of a judge in coding contests
  • At least one professional certification in the subject area
